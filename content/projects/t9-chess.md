## Problem

I switched to an Android-based keypad phone to cut my screen time, but I missed playing chess on it.

## Solution

So I built T9 Chess: A fully working chess app designed for a traditional T9 keypad. You can play locally against a friend or a chess engine and online against other players via the built in [Lichess](https://lichess.org) client.

{{< muted >}}Fun fact: **B08**, takes its name from the Pirc Defense — *B08* is the opening's [ECO code](/pirc/) and my favourite opening.{{< /muted >}}


## Try it

No touchscreen — navigate the board with the D-pad, just like on the real phone.
